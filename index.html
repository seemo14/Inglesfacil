Learning Tips

Listen Daily

Immerse yourself in English by listening to podcasts, music, or watching movies with subtitles for at least 15 minutes every day.

Read Aloud

Practice pronunciation by reading English texts aloud, recording yourself, and comparing with native speakers.

Find Practice Partners

Join language exchange groups online or in-person to practice conversation with other learners or native speakers.

Use Sticky Notes

Label objects around your home with their English names to build vocabulary through visual association.
